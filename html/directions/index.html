Directions¶

Dr. Rorden’s Neuropsychology Labs at University of South Carolina examine the behavioral difficulties people experience after brain injury. We are located in the city of Columbia, the state capitol of South Carolina. We are located in the Innovista, a short walk from the restaurants of the Vista, the state house and the historic University Horseshoe.
